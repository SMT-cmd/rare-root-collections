How Can We Help?

Find answers to frequently asked questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

Home / FAQ

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our collections, select your desired items, choose your size, and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready, click the cart icon and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping information and complete payment.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Please contact our customer service team immediately at support@rareroot.com with your order number. Once an order has been processed for shipping, we cannot make changes.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by clicking the "Track Order" link in the footer of our website and entering your order number.

What are the shipping options?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): FREE on orders over ₦100,000, otherwise ₦3,500
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): ₦7,500
  • Same Day Delivery (Lagos only): ₦10,000 for orders placed before 12 PM

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Shipping to African countries typically takes 5-10 business days, while shipping to Europe, North America, and Asia takes 7-14 business days. Customs fees may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times depend on your location and shipping method:

  • Lagos: 1-3 business days (Standard), Same day available
  • Other Nigerian cities: 3-5 business days
  • African countries: 5-10 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached. Sale items can be returned for store credit only. Underwear and swimwear cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, email us at returns@rareroot.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a return authorization and shipping instructions. Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

How do I find my size?

Each product page has a detailed size guide. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart. Our garments are designed with a relaxed, contemporary fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for an oversized look or down for a more fitted style.

What materials do you use?

We use premium, sustainably-sourced materials including 100% organic cotton, recycled polyester, and traditional African fabrics like Adire and Aso Oke. Each product description lists the specific materials used. We prioritize quality and sustainability in all our choices.

How should I care for my RARE ROOT garments?

To keep your garments looking their best:

  • Wash inside out in cold water
  • Use mild detergent
  • Air dry or tumble dry on low heat
  • Iron on low heat if needed
  • Do not bleach or dry clean

Each garment comes with specific care instructions on the label.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
  • Bank transfer
  • Paystack
  • Flutterwave
  • Cash on delivery (Lagos only, orders under ₦200,000)

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. We never store your full credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

Can I get a VAT invoice?

Yes, VAT invoices are available upon request. Please email your order number to support@rareroot.com and we'll send you a detailed VAT invoice within 24 hours.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer service team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
