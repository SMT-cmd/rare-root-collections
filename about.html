Our Story

Rooted in heritage, designed for the future. Discover the journey behind Nigeria's premier streetwear brand.

Home / About Us

From Vision to Reality

RARE ROOT was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create clothing that speaks to the soul of Nigeria while embracing global streetwear culture. Founded in 2020 in the heart of Lagos, we set out to bridge the gap between our rich heritage and contemporary fashion.

Every piece we create tells a story — of resilience, creativity, and the unbreakable spirit of the Nigerian people. We draw inspiration from the vibrant streets of Lagos, the intricate patterns of traditional Adire, and the bold energy of Afrobeats culture.

Our name reflects our mission: to be RARE in our approach to design, and always stay ROOTED in our identity. We don't just make clothes; we craft wearable art that celebrates who we are.

What We Stand For

Our core values guide everything we do, from design to delivery.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical production practices. Our commitment to the planet is woven into every garment we create.

Artisan Craft

We partner with local artisans and manufacturers, preserving traditional techniques while creating opportunities for Nigerian craftspeople.

Quality First

Every stitch matters. We use premium fabrics and rigorous quality control to ensure our pieces stand the test of time.

Community

RARE ROOT is more than a brand — it's a movement. We celebrate and support the creative community that inspires us daily.

Our Journey

Milestones that mark our growth from a dream to a movement.

2020

The Beginning

RARE ROOT was founded in Lagos with a small collection of 12 pieces, sold through Instagram and local pop-ups.

2021

First Flagship Store

Opened our first physical store in Lekki, Lagos, becoming a destination for streetwear enthusiasts across Nigeria.

2022

Pan-African Expansion

Launched shipping to Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa, bringing Nigerian streetwear to the continent.

2023

Global Recognition

Featured in Vogue Africa and Hypebeast, and collaborated with international artists on limited edition drops.

2024

The Future

Launched our sustainable collection and opened stores in London and New York, taking RARE ROOT global.
